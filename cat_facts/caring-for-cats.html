Diet
Cats like to consume several small meals a day. Make sure you're feading them cat food, as dog food and alot of human food isn't good for cats. Contarary to what people believe, they actually don't like milk.
Cleaning
Cats naturally make themselfs clean, and you rarely have to bathe a cat. The thing you have to worry about is hariballs, which you can ask your vet about
Health
